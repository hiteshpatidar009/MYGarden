Rooms

Standard room

Standard Room Description

The Standard Room comprises of 1 Double Bed or 2 Twin Beds, 2 Bedside Tables, a Desk & Chair. The room is furnished with wall to wall carpeting, trendy furnishings and a balcony. Our ultramodern glass bathroom is equipped with hairdryer, magnifying shaving and make up mirror as well as all the amenities you could possible need during your stay. A Complimentary Bottle of Wine, Fresh Fruit and Mineral Water, are provided on arrival. Electric current: 220 Volts. Smoking rooms & inter-connecting rooms are also available.

Suite room

Prambanan Suite Description

Our Prambanan Suite is a luxurious haven of contemporary. Enjoy a two-room suite perfectly equipped for comfort and convenience with an inviting living area, spacious bathroom and separate shower. Guests staying at our Prambanan Suite are entitled to enjoy the comfort and facilities at our Executive Lounge which include continental breakfast daily, daily refreshments, VIP express check-in and access to Gaharu Spa and Fitness, and use of the Business Center facility. In addition to complimentary food and beverage from the mini bar, guest stay at Prambanan Suite have the benefit to brew coffee from the pod coffee machine. Maximum Occupants: 8 people (4 adults & 4 children / 6 adults & 2 children) with additional extra beds

Executive room

Executive Room Description

The Executive Room offers elegant accommodations and exclusive benefits including continental breakfast daily, daily refreshments, VIP express check-in and use of the business center facility at The Executive Lounge. Maximum Occupants: 4 people (2 adults & 2 children / 3 adults & 1 child) with additional extra bed
